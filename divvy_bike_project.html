Motivation

In 2013, Divvy Bikes launched its service in the Chicago area. Divvy bikes provide convenient bike rental services that allow both one time user and subscribers to check out and return bikes from and to any divvy bike station.

This project aims at investigating user behavior variations according to the user characteristics available, seasonal variation of the service popularity as well as the association with neighborhood socio-economic attributes.
